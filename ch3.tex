\subsection{Timeline of the actions}\label{sec: timeline}
Figure \ref{fig: timeline} presents a placeholder sequence for a staged decision process. First, a planner chooses whether to activate an external signal source. Second, the planner selects a contract menu anticipating downstream behavior. Third, the signal source commits to a reporting rule. Fourth, latent quality is realized and both channels publish observations. Fifth, the planner sets a final policy variable. Sixth, end users update beliefs and choose actions. This placeholder narrative is intentionally generic and exists only to demonstrate figure integration, cross-referencing, and dissertation-safe formatting.
